ADVERSE EVENT SURVEILLANCE REPORT
Document ID: AER-0022
Drug: Semaglutide
Reporting Period: Q2 2024

SUMMARY
This post-marketing surveillance report summarizes adverse event data for Semaglutide collected through the FDA Adverse Event Reporting System (FAERS) and manufacturer safety databases. A total of 1153 adverse event reports were analyzed during the reporting period.

ADVERSE EVENT DISTRIBUTION
The following adverse events were reported:
  - muscle pain: 310 reports (26.89%), 60 serious
  - diarrhea: 20 reports (1.73%), 2 serious
  - nausea: 305 reports (26.45%), 2 serious
  - headache: 400 reports (34.69%), 22 serious
  - fatigue: 250 reports (21.68%), 33 serious
  - elevated liver enzymes: 343 reports (29.75%), 56 serious
  - constipation: 479 reports (41.54%), 35 serious

SIGNAL DETECTION
No new safety signals were identified during this reporting period. All adverse event rates remain within expected ranges based on clinical trial data.

RISK-BENEFIT ASSESSMENT
The overall benefit-risk profile of Semaglutide remains favorable based on current data. Continued monitoring is recommended.

DEMOGRAPHICS OF REPORTERS
- Age range: 25-81 years (median: 63)
- Sex: 50% Female, 45% Male, 1% Not reported
- Geographic distribution: 55% North America, 25% Europe, 11% Asia-Pacific
