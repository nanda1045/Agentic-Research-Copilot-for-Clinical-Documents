ADVERSE EVENT SURVEILLANCE REPORT
Document ID: AER-0019
Drug: Amlodipine
Reporting Period: Q1 2023

SUMMARY
This post-marketing surveillance report summarizes adverse event data for Amlodipine collected through the FDA Adverse Event Reporting System (FAERS) and manufacturer safety databases. A total of 8542 adverse event reports were analyzed during the reporting period.

ADVERSE EVENT DISTRIBUTION
The following adverse events were reported:
  - nausea: 235 reports (2.75%), 21 serious
  - constipation: 104 reports (1.22%), 1 serious
  - rash: 139 reports (1.63%), 27 serious
  - upper respiratory infection: 254 reports (2.97%), 7 serious

SIGNAL DETECTION
No new safety signals were identified during this reporting period. All adverse event rates remain within expected ranges based on clinical trial data.

RISK-BENEFIT ASSESSMENT
The overall benefit-risk profile of Amlodipine remains favorable based on current data. No regulatory action is recommended at this time.

DEMOGRAPHICS OF REPORTERS
- Age range: 18-74 years (median: 54)
- Sex: 58% Female, 44% Male, 1% Not reported
- Geographic distribution: 57% North America, 18% Europe, 13% Asia-Pacific
