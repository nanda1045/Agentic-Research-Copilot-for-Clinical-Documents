ADVERSE EVENT SURVEILLANCE REPORT
Document ID: AER-0020
Drug: Omeprazole
Reporting Period: Q2 2023

SUMMARY
This post-marketing surveillance report summarizes adverse event data for Omeprazole collected through the FDA Adverse Event Reporting System (FAERS) and manufacturer safety databases. A total of 7318 adverse event reports were analyzed during the reporting period.

ADVERSE EVENT DISTRIBUTION
The following adverse events were reported:
  - weight gain: 162 reports (2.21%), 27 serious
  - insomnia: 166 reports (2.27%), 3 serious
  - cough: 322 reports (4.4%), 12 serious
  - rash: 495 reports (6.76%), 97 serious
  - upper respiratory infection: 116 reports (1.59%), 20 serious

SIGNAL DETECTION
A new safety signal was identified for insomnia which exceeded the proportional reporting ratio (PRR) threshold of 2.0 (observed PRR: 2.68).

RISK-BENEFIT ASSESSMENT
The overall benefit-risk profile of Omeprazole remains favorable based on current data. Continued monitoring is recommended.

DEMOGRAPHICS OF REPORTERS
- Age range: 18-83 years (median: 64)
- Sex: 59% Female, 50% Male, 3% Not reported
- Geographic distribution: 51% North America, 22% Europe, 9% Asia-Pacific
