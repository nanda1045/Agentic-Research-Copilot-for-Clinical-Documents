ADVERSE EVENT SURVEILLANCE REPORT
Document ID: AER-0021
Drug: Dapagliflozin
Reporting Period: Q2 2023

SUMMARY
This post-marketing surveillance report summarizes adverse event data for Dapagliflozin collected through the FDA Adverse Event Reporting System (FAERS) and manufacturer safety databases. A total of 5132 adverse event reports were analyzed during the reporting period.

ADVERSE EVENT DISTRIBUTION
The following adverse events were reported:
  - dizziness: 348 reports (6.78%), 25 serious
  - upper respiratory infection: 227 reports (4.42%), 7 serious
  - diarrhea: 288 reports (5.61%), 14 serious
  - insomnia: 341 reports (6.64%), 19 serious
  - cough: 475 reports (9.26%), 34 serious
  - muscle pain: 433 reports (8.44%), 18 serious
  - constipation: 46 reports (0.9%), 0 serious

SIGNAL DETECTION
A new safety signal was identified for constipation which exceeded the proportional reporting ratio (PRR) threshold of 2.0 (observed PRR: 3.47).

RISK-BENEFIT ASSESSMENT
The overall benefit-risk profile of Dapagliflozin remains under review based on current data. Continued monitoring is recommended.

DEMOGRAPHICS OF REPORTERS
- Age range: 25-79 years (median: 62)
- Sex: 48% Female, 51% Male, 5% Not reported
- Geographic distribution: 65% North America, 29% Europe, 6% Asia-Pacific
