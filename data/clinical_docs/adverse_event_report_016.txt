ADVERSE EVENT SURVEILLANCE REPORT
Document ID: AER-0016
Drug: Metformin
Reporting Period: Q4 2023

SUMMARY
This post-marketing surveillance report summarizes adverse event data for Metformin collected through the FDA Adverse Event Reporting System (FAERS) and manufacturer safety databases. A total of 9048 adverse event reports were analyzed during the reporting period.

ADVERSE EVENT DISTRIBUTION
The following adverse events were reported:
  - elevated liver enzymes: 313 reports (3.46%), 17 serious
  - rash: 175 reports (1.93%), 15 serious
  - cough: 435 reports (4.81%), 11 serious
  - urinary tract infection: 152 reports (1.68%), 28 serious
  - peripheral edema: 240 reports (2.65%), 15 serious
  - joint pain: 394 reports (4.35%), 59 serious

SIGNAL DETECTION
No new safety signals were identified during this reporting period. All adverse event rates remain within expected ranges based on clinical trial data.

RISK-BENEFIT ASSESSMENT
The overall benefit-risk profile of Metformin remains favorable based on current data. Continued monitoring is recommended.

DEMOGRAPHICS OF REPORTERS
- Age range: 25-76 years (median: 61)
- Sex: 48% Female, 45% Male, 3% Not reported
- Geographic distribution: 69% North America, 23% Europe, 13% Asia-Pacific
