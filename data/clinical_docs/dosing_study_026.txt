DOSE-FINDING STUDY REPORT
Document ID: DFS-0026
Study Title: Dose-Response Evaluation of Atorvastatin in Hyperlipidemia

OBJECTIVE
To determine the optimal therapeutic dose of Atorvastatin for the treatment of Hyperlipidemia by evaluating efficacy and safety across multiple dose levels.

METHODS
This was a Phase IIb dose-ranging study. 343 patients with Hyperlipidemia were randomized to receive Atorvastatin at doses of 50mg, 100mg, or 150mg daily, or placebo, for 12 weeks.

PHARMACOKINETIC PROFILE
Atorvastatin demonstrated linear pharmacokinetics across the dose range studied:
- Tmax: 2.7 hours
- Half-life: 8.7 hours
- Bioavailability: 89%
- Steady-state achieved by Day 9
- No significant food effect observed

DOSE-RESPONSE RESULTS
50mg: Modest improvement over placebo (effect size: 0.11, p=0.124)
100mg: Significant improvement (effect size: 0.61, p=0.0083) — RECOMMENDED DOSE
150mg: Similar efficacy to 100mg but with increased adverse events (effect size: 0.5, p=0.007)

SAFETY BY DOSE
Treatment-emergent adverse events increased with dose:
- 50mg: 25% of patients
- 100mg: 24% of patients
- 150mg: 50% of patients
- Placebo: 10% of patients

RECOMMENDATION
The optimal dose of Atorvastatin for Hyperlipidemia is 100mg once daily, providing the best balance of efficacy and tolerability. Dose adjustment may be needed for patients with renal impairment (eGFR < 30 mL/min).
