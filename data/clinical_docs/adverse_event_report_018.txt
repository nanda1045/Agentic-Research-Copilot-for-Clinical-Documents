ADVERSE EVENT SURVEILLANCE REPORT
Document ID: AER-0018
Drug: Atorvastatin
Reporting Period: Q3 2022

SUMMARY
This post-marketing surveillance report summarizes adverse event data for Atorvastatin collected through the FDA Adverse Event Reporting System (FAERS) and manufacturer safety databases. A total of 4618 adverse event reports were analyzed during the reporting period.

ADVERSE EVENT DISTRIBUTION
The following adverse events were reported:
  - fatigue: 284 reports (6.15%), 48 serious
  - urinary tract infection: 363 reports (7.86%), 23 serious
  - muscle pain: 108 reports (2.34%), 6 serious
  - headache: 388 reports (8.4%), 61 serious
  - joint pain: 151 reports (3.27%), 23 serious

SIGNAL DETECTION
A new safety signal was identified for muscle pain which exceeded the proportional reporting ratio (PRR) threshold of 2.0 (observed PRR: 2.65).

RISK-BENEFIT ASSESSMENT
The overall benefit-risk profile of Atorvastatin remains under review based on current data. Continued monitoring is recommended.

DEMOGRAPHICS OF REPORTERS
- Age range: 29-87 years (median: 54)
- Sex: 48% Female, 36% Male, 1% Not reported
- Geographic distribution: 67% North America, 24% Europe, 7% Asia-Pacific
